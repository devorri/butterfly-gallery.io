"Echoes of Influence"

Artists: Atasha Kristen Pelaez and Trisha Camille A. Venzon

Description: This painting shows how caring has a lasting impact on a child's journey, mirroring the idea that tiny acts have big results. A child appears to be reflecting as two contrasting worlds emerge around him. To the left, abandoned objects, shattered glass, and a man with a wounded face depict a journey shaped by disregard or criticism. An optimistic scene to the right features a gold medal, a dazzling report card, and a smiling figure, signifying supporting mentorship and drive. It is possible to influence others' growth and thought processes by being mindful of your interactions with them, even as a student. Every action matters, regardless of whether it pertains to your interactions with mentors, family, or peers. No matter where you are in your voyage, it is important to be aware of the ways in which you affect the world around you. Even the smallest amount is significant.
