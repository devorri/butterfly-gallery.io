"Paths Unchosen, Destinies Forged"

Artists: Atasha Kristen Pelaez and Trisha Camille A. Venzon

Description: This painting's emotional depth and simplicity effectively communicate the butterfly impact concept. The opposing selections of "Nursing" and "Pharmacy" above her signify the character's position at a turning point. Her contemplative stare and the faint thinking bubble elicit the internal monologue of doubting past choices and their effects on her life. The focus on her face represents the gravity of her decision, while the subdued background and serene colours capture a moment of reflection. It seems as though the painting captures the instant when one choice sets the route for an unforeseen journey, making her consider other options. It challenges viewers to consider their own "what-ifs" and the significant effects of seemingly insignificant choices.
