"Woven Solace"

Artists: Atasha Kirsten Pelaez and Trisha Camille A. Venzon

Description: Captures the essence of finding solace and healing in one's own element. The image of a girl, alone with her crochet hook and yarn, symbolizes the comfort and peace she finds in the act of creating. As she weaves intricate patterns and stitches, she also weaves herself back together, finding solace in her own company and the therapeutic nature of crocheting. The vibrant colors and delicate details of the crochet work represent the beauty and strength that can be found in solitude and self-care. This artwork serves as a reminder to embrace our own unique elements and find comfort and healing within them.
